My predictions for crypto and blockchain mass adoption

Crypto and blockchain in 2022

DeFi

  • DeFi ecosystems on other chains
  • New ATH TVL in DeFi as digital native millennials think about generating wealth and Gen Z entering the workforce start to explore investment opportunities
  • BTC market share continues to shrink compared to other chains
  • More DeFi 2.0 type algorithmic stablecoins

NFTs

  • Continuing the tokenization of everything: credentials, healthcare, real estate, tickets to any events, luxury goods, etc.
  • NFT ecosystems on chains outside of Ethereum
  • NFT winter? : bluechip projects of this cycle become apparent, long-term players with vision keep building

DAOs

  • More experimental DAOs with protocol owned liquidity
  • Traditional companies experimenting with DAOs within different areas of their business?
  • More DAO tooling

Large Corporations

  • Legacy companies get serious about creating digital IP, hiring talent, and forming digital asset teams
  • Partnerships with crypto platforms, enabling crypto payments, being more proactive in cross-chain interoperability

Governments

  • Get more serious about regulation
  • Explore digital currencies and stablecoins
  • Bitcoin as legal tender in more countries?

Digital identities, metaverse, & gaming

  • The race to onboard users to metaverse platforms continues, probably mostly centralized companies building decentralized platforms
  • Increase in blockchain domain names fuelled by the explosion of NFTs, metaverse opportunities, and DAOs

And a ton of other stuff that nobody will see coming :)

General mass adoption of crypto and blockchain

  • Maturation in blockchain systems: well-tested, tried and true use cases
  • Adoption across all ages, cultures, nations, ethnicities, occupations, genders, etc.
  • Digital identities and assets across healthcare, insurance, business operations, real estate, legal systems, etc.
  • Much more lateral crypto transacting and wealth continuing to flow into crypto ecosystems
  • Many countries have issued their own digital currencies or adopted some form of a regulated stablecoin
  • More native blockchain use cases that are not possible within Big Tech
  • NFT winters allow blue chip projects to shine

Blockchains integrated into the fabric of society

  • Smart contracts replace middlemen for most transactions
  • Many transactions happen in near real-time
  • Property rights are (mostly) on a digital, globally accessible, decentralized database
  • It becomes normal to invest in crypto
